About the Author

James "Mr. Speaker" Sears

Veteran leader - Spoken-word poet - Author & mentor

James "Mr. Speaker" Sears is a highly decorated retired combat veteran and former U.S. Army officer, known for blending leadership, faith, history, and lived experience into spoken-word and poetry. He's also an event host, mentor, former adjunct professor, and track coach, and is listed as a Department of the Army Civilian.

Across his work, Sears explores personal growth, relationships, and purpose - moving between motivational storytelling and reflective verse with a strong service-and-leadership backbone.

Location: Louisville, Kentucky, USA (originally from)
